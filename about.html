About Us

S & D Auto Repair, LLC is an independent family owned and operated service and diagnostic facility that opened in 2010. Our mission is to provide a quality automotive repair atmosphere for the families within the Centralia, Missouri and surrounding areas. We do this by providing the utmost quality in services while keeping our costs to a minimum.

The Family

Owned and operated by Stewart Burton and his family members, this truly is a family oriented business. I want to make sure that your family is as safe on the roads as I would want my own family members to be.
